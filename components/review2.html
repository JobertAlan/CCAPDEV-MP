Cassalu

Came in for a late lunch, so not a lot of diners. Cheerful ambience, clean, comfortable seating, and efficient staff. I was looking for soup, but they didn’t have it, so i ordered rice and tofu with mushrooms instead. Servings are good for two, and leaving room for coffee or dessert is not a bad idea. Tofu was crispy, and the sauce savory but the mushrooms were more of a garnish. The bangus could be more boneless, the garlic rice was bland and a little wet, and the yang chow fried rice was missing it’s characteristic flavor. Fortunately, the Carrot cake was moist, with thick cream cheese and a generous walnut side crust. The Blueberry cheesecake was not crumbly or too sweet. Both perfect with their Cafe Americano, which was not bitter.
2
